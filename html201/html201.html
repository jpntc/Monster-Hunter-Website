Why You Should Play Monster Hunter

Monster hunter is one of the Best Series of Games Ever Created.




What Makes Monster Hunter Special?

To start Monster Hunter is a series of games that have been made since 2010. In any series you play, there is always one thing in common, immerssion. The creators go above and beyond to make the atmosphere of the game feel as immersive as possible through the amazing soundtacts, detailed environments and monsters. The user interface and systems also look and feel spectacular. No other game in this specific genre could compare to it unless they copy similar elements, yet, even then, you will see that MH is a cut above.


Downsides

The downsides to the Monster Hunter series are

  • Graphics Quality
  • Limited Weapon Types
  • Limited Number of Players in Online Multiplayer




Graphics Quality: The graphics for the games decrease in quality from 2011 and lower. This is acceptable since gaming graphics are less detailed the earlier you go in time. However, if you can play with this then you will see that the earlier games are still that great. It is recommended to play a more modern game from the series with breathtaking graphics, you will definitely want to play the earlier games regardless.





Limited Number of Weapon Types: Similarly to the graphics quality decreasing as you go earlier in the series of games, the number of weapons available also decrease. This also applies for the weapon moveset, skills, and other features. This is not limiting in the modern games or games that are after 2014, there are 14 different weapon types, and each of these have dozens of elements that can be combined with armor skills for different playstyles.





Limited Number of Players in Online Multiplayer Another major disadvantage for the game is that it only allows a maximum of 4 players to take part in an online quest. This remained the same throughout every game of the series for various reasons. One major one being that quests will be too fast and easy.
